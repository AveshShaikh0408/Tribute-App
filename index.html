Mr. Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist

  • 1869 - Birth at Porbandar.
  • 1888 - Reached Southampton, England.
  • 1893 - Reached Durban, Natal.
  • 1894 - Founded the 'Natal Indian Congress'.
  • 1917 - Sabarmati Ashram established.
  • 1928 - Boycott of Simon Commission.
  • 1917 - Sabarmati Ashram established.
  • 1948 - Cremated on the banks of the Yamuna by Ramdas.
